original website: FishWife.com

I chose this website because I found it interesting, different, and unnecessarily unintuitive.

Usability Issues:
  • The norm is for menu bars to be at the top or left side of a page, so users may have to look harder to find this menu bar at the bottom
  • I intuitively want to scroll down on this page, maybe because the menu bar is on the bottom, but scrolling just leads to the photo moving up a little bit then back down
  • The large background photo that takes up the most of the page is too low quality for its size—it looks pixelated and out of focus
  • The big “FISHWIFE” text in the middle of the photo could be hard to read for some people—the font is not the most clear/legible and it is against a fully saturated photo
  • The “Seafood・Restaurant・Bar” text under the “FISHWIFE” text in the middle of the photo do not match either of the other fonts on the page and has a gray border around the letters which makes the text look clickable (their location in the hierarchy of the page might also be contributing to the clickable aspect)
  • The most common use of restaurant websites is to view the menu, which is not given any focus or emphasis on this webpage
  • The “FISHWIFE” text at the bottom of the page breaks up the menu bar, unintuitively acts as a home-button, and is repetitive considering the exact same text directly above
Usability with WebAIM WAVE:
  • Detected very low contrast between the “Seafood・Restaurant・Bar” text and the background colors
  • The “FISHWIFE” text at the bottom of the page is a linked image without alternative text, which means that there is no information about the function of the link for a screen reader to provide for a user
  • The language of the document (“lang” value in html) is unidentified/invalid, which prohibits translation and correct screen reading

I agree with the issue of low contrast and although I didn’t think of the other two issues because I have never used a screen reader or built a website with alternative text, I now know that alternative text and language identification are very important and need to be added.
